Features

As participants in recreational sports leagues, we were frustrated by how user unfriendly league management apps can be. As software developers, we decided to fix the problem.

Easy To Use

You're busy and you don't have time to learn a complex piece of software. TopShelf sticks to the basics without making compromises.

Modern

By using modern technologies, we are able to reduce overhead and pass the savings on to you.

Mobile Friendly

Analytics show that 70% of traffic on similar apps comes from mobile devices. Building a mobile-friendly interface is one of our key priorities.

Statistics

Every sports fanatic loves statistics. We have spent hundreds of hours designing and implmenting workflows that will allow you to track statistics that your players will love.

Team standings and statistics are automatically calculated based on values entered in scoresheets.
